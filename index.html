Section 4 · Healthcare & Long-Term Care
Protecting your health and your wealth
Healthcare and long-term care events represent the single largest financial risk in retirement. Long-term care costs average $40,000–$50,000 per year for custodial care not covered by Medicare or supplements, lasting an average of 2–3 years. Over 50% of people will need some form of long-term care. Planning ahead now preserves your options and keeps control in your hands.
